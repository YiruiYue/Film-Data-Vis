Big Fish Eating Away Small Fish?

A healthy industry should have many participants to compete with each other. In this case, is film industry health?

  • Major film corporations are: Disney, WB, Universal, 20th Fox, Columbia, and Paramount.
  • Number of films produced by major six corporations has been increasing from less than 20% to more that 25% during the past 30 years.
  • Major film corporations seems to have more and more market share in the future, which is not healthy for the film industry.
